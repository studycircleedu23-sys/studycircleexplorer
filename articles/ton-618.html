🌌 TON 618

Meet one of the largest and most massive black holes ever discovered in the known universe.

16 July 2026 6 min read Study Circle Explorer

What is TON 618?

TON 618 is an ultra-luminous quasar powered by one of the most massive black holes ever discovered. It lies approximately 10.4 billion light-years from Earth and shines so brightly that it outshines its entire host galaxy.

Astronomers estimate that the black hole at the center of TON 618 has a mass of around 66 billion times that of our Sun, making it one of the largest known black holes in the observable universe.

⭐ Incredible Fact

If TON 618 replaced the Sun, its event horizon would extend far beyond the orbit of Neptune.

Why is TON 618 So Bright?

TON 618 is classified as a quasar, meaning enormous amounts of gas and dust are falling toward its black hole. As this material spirals inward, it becomes extremely hot and releases tremendous amounts of energy, making the quasar visible across billions of light-years.

This brilliant glow comes from the surrounding accretion disk—not from the black hole itself, which remains invisible.

How Big is TON 618?

TON 618 is one of the largest known black holes in the observable universe. Its estimated mass is about 66 billion times the mass of the Sun. The event horizon alone is so enormous that light would take several days to travel across it.

Unlike stellar black holes formed from collapsing stars, TON 618 belongs to the category of ultra-massive black holes that power extremely bright quasars.

🚀 Mind-Blowing Fact

If our Solar System were placed inside the event horizon of TON 618, every planet—including Neptune—would still lie deep inside the black hole.

Why Do Scientists Study TON 618?

TON 618 helps astronomers understand how supermassive black holes grow to extraordinary sizes. It also provides clues about the early universe because its light has traveled for more than 10 billion years before reaching Earth.

By studying quasars like TON 618, scientists learn about galaxy evolution, black hole formation, and the behavior of matter under extreme gravity.

Amazing Facts About TON 618

  • ⭐ Located about 10.4 billion light-years from Earth.
  • ⭐ Estimated mass of 66 billion Suns.
  • ⭐ Classified as an ultra-luminous quasar.
  • ⭐ One of the largest black holes ever discovered.
  • ⭐ Surrounded by a brilliant accretion disk that shines brighter than an entire galaxy.

Quick Summary

TON 618 is an ultra-massive black hole powering one of the brightest known quasars. Its incredible size and distance make it one of the most fascinating objects in modern astronomy and an important target for scientific research.
