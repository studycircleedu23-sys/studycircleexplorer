🌌 AMAZING SPACE FACTS

A Day on Venus Is Longer Than Its Year

Venus takes longer to rotate once on its axis than it takes to orbit the Sun.


Uranus Rotates Sideways

Unlike most planets, Uranus rotates on its side with an axial tilt of about 98 degrees.


There Are More Trees on Earth Than Stars in the Milky Way

Earth has roughly 3 trillion trees, while the Milky Way contains about 100–400 billion stars.


A Teaspoon of Neutron Star Matter Is Extremely Heavy

Just a teaspoon of neutron star material would weigh billions of tons on Earth.


The Sun Contains 99.8% of the Solar System's Mass

Nearly all the mass in our solar system is concentrated inside the Sun.
